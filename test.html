Here is an example of an algorithm for making a pot of tea.



Select CSS: justdoit.css (default) or justdoit-clear.css.

Does kettle contain water?
Fill the kettle.
Plug the kettle into the power point and switch it on.
Is teapot empty?
Empty the teapot.
Place tea leaves in the teapot.
Wait for 15 seconds.
Is the water in the kettle boiling?
Switch the kettle off.
Pour water from the kettle into the teapot.


This example was created using constructor.
